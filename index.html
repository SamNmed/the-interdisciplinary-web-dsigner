The Text

When it comes to singing text is very important and prenusication. It is important to open as much space in the mouth for resonence so you must restructure your speech to work best with that while still being legable. Speech is structured in phrases because as humans we breath and being able to maintain breath for longer phrases and expending and utalizing all breath on short phrases. A large part of music is understanding the weight of words in a phrase. What keywords should be brought out in a phrase?
